Top Movies to watch on OTT's

Top Movies to watch on OTT's

1) IT

IMDB Rating: 7.3
Available in: Netflix
Audio Languages: English
Genre: Thriller/Mystery
Running Time: 135 minutes

It (also known as It: Chapter One) is an American supernatural horror film. The film tells the story of seven children who are terrorized by the eponymous being, only to face their own personal demons in the process.



2) Super 30

IMDB Rating: 8.2
Available in: Hotstar (Premium)
Audio Languages: Hindi
Genre: Drama
Running Time: 135 minutes

Super 30 is an Indian biographical film directed by Vikas Bahl, based on the life of mathematician Anand Kumar and his educational program of the same name.



3) Now You See Me

IMDB Rating: 7.3
Available in: Netflix
Audio Languages: English
Genre: Mystery/Crime
Running Time: 115 minutes

Four street magicians, Daniel, Merritt, Henley and Jack, ransack a huge amount of money belonging to insurance baron Arthur Tressler while being chased by FBI agent Dylan and Interpol agent Alma.



4) Mission Mangal

IMDB Rating: 6.5
Available in: Hotstar
Audio Languages: Hindi
Genre: Drama and Fantasy
Running Time: 127 minutes

A group of scientists at ISRO battle in their personal and professional lives and work tirelessly towards their only motive, the Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM). It is lightly based on the life of scientists at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) who contributed to the Mars Orbiter Mission, India's first interplanetary expedition.



5) Forrest Gump

IMDB Rating: 8.8
Available in: Netflix
Audio Languages: English
Genre: Comedy Drama
Running Time: 142 minutes

Forrest Gump, a man with a low IQ, joins the army for service where he meets Dan and Bubba. However, he cannot stop thinking about his childhood sweetheart Jenny Curran, whose life is messed up.



6) Final Destination

IMDB Rating: 6.7
Available in: Hotstar and Amazon Prime Video
Audio Languages: English
Genre: Mystery and Thriller
Running Time: 98 minutes

Alex is boarding a plane to France on a school trip, when he suddenly gets a premonition that the plane will explode. Shortly after Alex, a group of students, and his teacher are thrown off the plane, to their horror, the plane does in fact explode. Alex must now work out Death's plan, as each of the survivors falls victim. Whilst trying to prevent the next death, Alex must also dodge the FBI, who believe that he caused the explosion.



7) A Quiet Place

IMDB Rating: 7.5
Available in: Amazon Prime Video
Audio Languages: English, Hindi, Tamil and Telugu
Genre: Thriller
Running Time: 91 minutes

A family struggles for survival in a world where most humans have been killed by blind but noise-sensitive creatures. They are forced to communicate in sign language to keep the creatures at bay.



8) Andhadoon

IMDB Rating: 8.4
Available in: Jio Cinemas
Audio Languages: Hindi
Genre: Thriller and Mystery
Running Time: 138 minutes

Akash, a piano player pretending to be visually-impaired. A series of mysterious events changes the life of a blind pianist. Unwittingly becomes entangled in a number of problems as he witnesses the murder of a former film actor who must now report that crime that he should technically know nothing of.



9) Harry Potter Series

Available in: Amazon Prime Video
Audio Languages: English
Genre: Sci-Fi/Fantasy, Family, Action and Adventure
Running Time: 1179 minutes

When Harry Potter learns on his eleventh birthday that he is, in fact, a wizard, he is quickly swept up into the spellbinding world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry alongside new best friends, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. He soon discovers, though, that there is a much darker side to the wizarding world than any of them could have imagined.

The Magic Is All Here in the Complete 8-Film Collection. The collection includes:

1) Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

2) Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

3) Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

4) Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

5) Harry Potter and the Order of Phoenix

6) Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

7) Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 1

8) Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2



10) Chhichhore

IMDB Rating: 8.4
Available in: Amazon Prime Video
Audio Languages: Hindi
Genre: Drama
Running Time: 143 minutes

Following a group of friends from university as they progress into middle-age life and go their own separate ways. The plot follows the life of seven friends from 1992 to present day.
