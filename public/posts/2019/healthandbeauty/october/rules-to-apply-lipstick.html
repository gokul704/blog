Rules to apply Lipstick flawlessly

A superbly applied lipstick can enhance your glow many times but other way is also possible, a minor slip-up can ruin your entire look. Always follow some rules while applying lipstick as given.

Here are the rules:

Rule 1: Always pick right shade of lipstick that suits your look according to your skin tone.

You know your lip colour, right? Best lipstick shade is a shade that is darker than your natural lip colour. If you are not an expert, do not go for orange or brown shades, including corals. They look good on very few people.

Rule 2: Pick your most ideal approach to apply it.

  • Some say perfect way to apply lipstick is to use lip brush. But let me tell you, not everyone is comfortable with lip brush. To use lip brush in a proper way is also a talent.
  • Some use their fore finger or center finger to apply lipstick.
  • Some apply lipstick directly from tube.

    • Among all these, no one but only YOU can choose which works best for you.

    Rule 3: Prior to lipstick, apply liner as base.

    Lipstick will last longer if you fill in your lips with the liner first. If you are using a light lipstick, try a nude lip liner. Then slick your lipstick on top.

    Rule 4: Trick to keep your lipstick off your teeth.

    After applying lipstick, take your forefinger and pop it in your mouth and after that haul it out. The excess lipstick will fall off on your finger rather than staying on your teeth.

    Rule 5: Enjoy yourself!

    The best accessory for a perfect lip colour? Confidence! Since now you have a cheat sheet for choosing and applying your best lipstick, show it off with your delightful smile and have some good time.
